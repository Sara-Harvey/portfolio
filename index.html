Mortality Report

New Yorkers can easily browse a 128-page city report on death rate by neighborhood. PDFs aren't formatted for data extraction, but Ruby's PDF::Reader::Turtletext gem and slice function made it possible to organize the raw data. Visit the report on the web.

@NewNewRomBot1 is a Ruby Twitter bot that mashes lyrics from Duran Duran, Boy George, Flock of Seagulls and other acts from the New Romantic/New Wave early 80s. It tweets daily at@NewNewRomBot1! Or visit on the web.
